ABOUT CHALK

BUILT FOR THE ONES WHO TRAIN

Not the ones who perform for cameras.

WHY CHALK EXISTS

Athletes post on Instagram, TikTok, and X — but sports content gets buried under everything else. Recruiting is hit or miss. Communities are scattered across WhatsApp groups and Discord servers. There's no LinkedIn for athletes, and the recruiting window closes fast. Chalk was born to fix that — one platform where athletes find their community, get discovered, and organize the games that matter.
