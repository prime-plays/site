QUESTIONS?

When does Chalk launch?

We're in early development, talking to athletes first. We'll announce launch dates to waitlist members before anyone else.

Is Chalk free?

Chalk will always have a free tier for athletes. We believe access to a merit-based platform shouldn't cost anything.

What sports are supported?

We're starting with the most popular athletic disciplines and expanding based on community demand. If your sport isn't listed, select 'Other' — we want to hear from you.

How is Chalk different from Instagram or TikTok?

Those platforms are built for everyone. Chalk is built specifically for athletes. Content rises on merit, not followers. There are no algorithms deciding who gets seen.
