The Platform

Built for the Ones Who Show Up

Athletes post on Instagram, TikTok, and X, but sports content gets buried under everything else. Recruiting is hit or miss. Communities are scattered across WhatsApp groups and Discord servers. Chalk brings it all together in one place built specifically for athletes.

Features

Communities

Sport-specific communities with posts, threads, votes, and challenges. Find your tribe. Like Reddit, but built for athletes.

Recruiting

Athletic profiles with stats, rankings, and match history. Get discovered by scouts and coaches. Like LinkedIn, but for athletes.

Matches & Events

Host and join matches, book courts, find pickup games. Everything your WhatsApp group does, but better. All in one place.
