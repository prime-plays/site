Features

Everything an Athlete Needs

Communities. Recruiting. Events. Challenges. One platform.

01

Communities

Sport-specific communities where athletes actually belong. Posts, threads, upvotes, and challenges. Like Reddit, but built from the ground up for sports. Your content rises because the community says it's good, not because you gamed an algorithm.

  • Sport-specific communities and discussions
  • Merit-based feed with upvote/downvote
  • Hot, New, and Top sorting
  • Find your tribe across any sport
02

Recruiting

Instagram reels might get you noticed, but they don't get you hired. Chalk gives athletes a professional profile with stats, rankings, and matches played, and lets scouts and coaches discover talent systematically. Like LinkedIn, but the stakes are higher.

  • Athletic profiles with stats and rankings
  • Scout and coach discovery tools
  • Direct messaging that matters
  • Performance-based visibility, not follower count
03

Matches & Events

No more scattered WhatsApp groups to find a pickup game or book a court. Host and join matches, organize tournaments, and create polls. Everything your sports community coordinates, now in one place that's actually built for it.

  • Host and join matches and pickup games
  • Book courts and organize events
  • Polls, scheduling, and coordination
  • Replace fragmented group chats
04

Challenges & Leaderboards

Weekly sport-specific challenges that test real skill. Film your attempt, submit it, let the community vote. Live leaderboards track where you stand, globally and per sport. Real competition, no politics.

  • Weekly sport-specific challenges
  • Community voting on submissions
  • Global and per-sport leaderboards
  • Performance tracking over time
